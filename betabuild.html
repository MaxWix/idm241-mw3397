Triggers:

  • hover on book
  • hover off book
  • click on "want to read" drop-down

    • Rules:

  • On hover of book: a drop shadow will fade in around the entire div and the book cover img will scale up slighty (zoom in)
  • Off hover of book: the drop shadow will fade out and the book will scale back down to it's original size
  • On click of "want to read" drop down: a drop down menu that is the same width as the "want to read" button will slide down and the different options will fade in
