Idm 241 Final Description - Max Wix

https://www.goodreads.com/choiceawards/best-books-2022

Triggers:

  1. hover on/off container
  2. hover on/off "want to read" drop-down
  3. hover on/off stars
  4. on click of "want to read" menu item
  5. on click of stars

Rules:

  1. On hover on whole container: box fades to a darker grey and cursor will turn to pointer.
  2. Off hover off container: boxs returns to original grey and cursor will return to default.
  3. On hover on "want to read" drop down: a drop down menu with "want to read", "currently reading", and "read" options, that is the same width as the "want to read" button will slide down and fade in and cursor will turn to pointer.
  4. On hover off "want to read" drop down: the dropdown menu will slide up and fade out to its originial, hidden position and cursor will return to default.
  5. On hover on "want to read" menu item: div behind menu item text will fade to gray and cursor will turn to pointer.
  6. On hover off "want to read" menu item: div behind menu item text will fade back original color and cursor will return to default.
  7. On hover on stars: Stars will fade in with yellow, the number of stars that are filled from left to right directly coorelates with which star is being hovered over (1st star is on the left, last star is on the right). Specific star that is being hovered over will scale up and cursor will turn to pointer.
  8. on hover off stars: All stars that were filled with yellow will fade back to grey, specific star that was being hovered on will scale back down to original size and cursor will return to default.
  9. on click of "want to read" menu item: the dropdown menu will slide up and fade out to its originial, hidden position. Database updated based on menu item selected.
  10. on click of stars: stars that are filled from hover will remain filled. Star that is clicked on will scale down slightly and then scale back to hover size and remain that size (bounce animation). Database updated based on
