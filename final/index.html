Good Reads Best-Books Microinteraction

Fiction

  • Want to Read
  • Currently Reading
  • Read
Rate this book

Triggers:

  • Hover on/off the book
  • Hover on/off the "Reading Status" drop-down
  • Hover on/off "Reading Status" menu items
  • On click of "Reading Status" menu item
  • Hover on/off the stars when empty
  • Hover on/off the stars when selection has already been made (some or all stars are filled)
  • On click of Stars
  • On click of a new star when a selection has already been made (some or all stars are filled
  • On click of a star that was previously selected

Rules:

  • On hover on the book: A white drop shadow fades in 4 pixels down and 4 pixels right from the book
  • Off hover off container: The white drops shadow fades out back into the book
  • On hover on "Reading Status" drop down: a drop down menu with "want to read", "currently reading", and "read" options that is the same width as the "Reading Status" button will slide down and fade in.
  • On hover off "Reading Status" drop down: the dropdown menu will slide up and fade out to its original, hidden position
  • On hover on "Reading Status" menu item: div behind menu item text will fade to gray and cursor will turn to pointer.
  • On hover off "Reading Status" menu item: div behind menu item text will fade back original color and cursor will return to default.
  • on click of "Reading Status" menu item: the dropdown menu will slide up and fade out to its original, hidden position. The text of the specific menu item clicked will replace the "Reading Status" text. Menu item selection logged to local storage and database updated based on menu item selected.
  • On hover on stars when previously empty: Stars will fade in with yellow, the number of stars that are filled from left to right directly correlates with which star is being hovered over (1st star is on the left, last star is on the right). Specific star that is being hovered over will scale up and cursor will turn to pointer.
  • on hover off stars when previously empty: All stars that were filled with yellow will fade back to grey, specific star that was being hovered on will scale back down to original size and cursor will return to default.
  • hover on the stars when selection has already been made (some or all stars are filled): Star being hovered on will fade in yellow if it was previously unselected. The number of stars that are filled from left to right directly correlates with which star is being hovered over (1st star is on the left, last star is on the right). Any star that was previously selected to the right of the star being hovered on, if applicable, will fade to its original gray. Specific star that is being hovered over will scale up and cursor will turn to pointer.
  • hover off the stars when selection has already been made (some or all stars are filled): All stars that were previously selected to the right of the star being hovered on, if applicable, will fade back to its previously selected yellow. The specific star that was being hovered on will scale back down to its original size and cursor will return to default.
  • on click of stars: The stars that are filled from hover will remain filled. Star that is clicked on will scale down to original size when hovered off. Star selection logged to local storage and database updated based on Star selected.
  • On click of a new star when a selection has already been made (some or all stars are filled): The Stars that are filled from hover will remain filled. Star that is clicked on will scale down to original size when hovered off. Star selection logged to local storage and database updated based on Star selected.
  • On click of a star that was previously selected: All stars that were filled with yellow will fade back to grey. Lack of star selection logged to local storage and database.

  • no loops

Extra Credit
